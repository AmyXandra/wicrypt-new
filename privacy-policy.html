Privacy Policy

About Wicrypt

This privacy policy governs the use of this software application and service “Wicrypt” (hereinafter referred to as “APP”) that was created by Wicrypt Tech Limited. The App allows its users to get paid while sharing their mobile or device’s network. It leverages blockchain technology and allows users to get paid per kilobyte of their data consumed by a beneficiary. Wicrypt has an inbuilt wallet that verifies that the beneficiary has enough cryptocurrency in order to make use of its services. A user is at liberty to set their price range per kilobyte of data consumed. The App automatically cuts off a beneficiary when his money is exhausted and as well allows automatic connection between a user and a beneficiary once the beneficiary has enough funds to utilize the App’s services.

Wicrypt’s has a different interface designed for individuals and businesses to enable each category make the most of the App’s features.
