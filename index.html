Wicrypt Features

Create A Chain Of Hotspot Networks

Create your own hotspot network, or chain of hotspot networks and offer your community secure access to your high-speed internet for free or at whatever rates you choose.

Collect Information

Collect rich, clean, first-party data when users connect to your hotspot network. Serve advertising, create quizzes, surveys and forms for your network users.

Monitor Data Usage

Add friends, family, employees, or customers to your account, allocate data to them, monitor overall consumption and track individual usage over time.

Switch Providers

Free yourself from constantly switching devices for a good connection. Wicrypt devices allow you to move between mobile and fibre network providers easily.

Easy Redemption

Receive payments into your wallet account when other users connect to your hotspot network and cash out all of your earnings on any of your bank accounts.

Recharge Your Data

Fund your wallet and automatically top-up your data balance at wholesale rates. Gain access to great deals nationwide from a variety of internet service providers.

Partners

Wicrypt is tested, trusted, and used by top businesses in Africa and beyond.
