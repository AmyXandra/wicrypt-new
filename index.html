What is Wicrypt?

Wicrypt Features

All you need to stay connected

Flexible Connections

You can easily switch to a different provider wherever network is unstable or unavailable.

Versatile Mi-Fi /Router Connection

Set up your wicrypt account, put in any micro sim card of your choice to your wicrypt router and start sharing data.

Wicrypt ad-support

Wicrypt allows you to white label and advertise on your sign-on landing page affording your business more visibility.

Payment

Wicrypt leverages technology that allows you to receive payment per gigabyte(GB) of your Wi-Fi data consumed by user.

Data Allocation

Share data with your community so they can stay connected everytime. You can Manage and monitor data usage of all users.

Wicrypt is connection made easy

Wicrypt is a connectivity service that allows businesses and government-led agencies to share internet access to the people who need them. Wicrypt allows you to create and build paid or ad-supported network to enable affordable internet access in local communities.
