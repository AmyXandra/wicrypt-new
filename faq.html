Frequently Asked Questions

Browse the topics below to find what you are looking for.

Does Wicrypt sell data?

Wicrypt provides a platform for ease of sharing data. Businesses, Agents and Individuals get this data from their preferred network providers.

How do I fund my wallet?

You can fund your wallet in a few easy steps:

  1. Open your Wicrypt app and tap on the “Fund Wallet” button.
  2. Select your preferred method of funding.
  3. Follow the steps.
  4. Voila! Your money will reflect in your Wicrypt wallet.

Is Wicrypt Available Nationwide?

Wicrypt can work anywhere in the world. The service is currently only available in Nigeria. However, we intend to expand to other regions as we grow.

Is The Wi-Fi Fast?

The WiFi speed depends on your proximity to the hotspot provider and the speed of the network provider.

How Do I Connect My Mobile Phone Using A Voucher?

Wicrypt allows you to login and enjoy Wi-Fi even if you don’t have the Wicrypt app. Simply purchase a voucher and follow this steps:

  1. Turn on your mobile phone Wi-Fi. Connect to the Wicrypt hotspot near you. Once connected the router login page will open automatically.
  2. On the login page , tap “Login with Voucher”.
  3. Enter the voucher code and the phone number you send in purchasing your voucher. Then tap on the “Login” button.
  4. Once logged in successfully, you will see a success page displaying your data usage.

How do I know which wi-Fi is being shared from a Wicrypt hotspot?

"Wicrypt hotspots are typically named in this format
“XXXX.wc”(eg.NetworkName.wc)."

Can the hotspot provider access my browsing data or history?

Your secrets are safe. No hotspot provider on Wicrypt will be able to monitor your internet activity.

As an individual, how can I earn on Wicrypt?

It's simple:

  • Download the app
  • Create an account
  • Load your account up with data
  • Set your billing rates
  • Share your hotspot and start earning

What's the billing rate?

You have the freedom to set your billing rates. However, there is a floor and a ceiling (minimum and maximum) for rates you can set to avoid inflation of prices.

Do I need to be a business to do this?

You do not have to be a business to share your WiFi and bill people. Whether you are an individual, an agent, or a super agent, you can share your hotspot. As long as you have a Wicrypt account, you can share your hotspot and start earning

Is Wicrypt available worldwide?

Wicrypt is currently designed to work in Nigeria. However, we intend to expand to other regions as we grow.

How will I be credited for users using my WiFi?

Users will only be able to connect to your internet if they have enough Wicrypt Credits (WC) in their wallet. Wicrypt automatically debits users per GB used and credits your wallet.

Can the WiFi get good and speedy signal anywhere?

The WiFi speed depends on the network provider you choose and the proximity of your beneficiaries to your location. However, the Wicrypt router increases the range of your hotspot signal.

How do I withdraw my money?

You can withdraw your earnings in a few easy steps:

  1. Tap on the “Withdraw” icon on your Wicrypt app.
  2. Choose your preferred currency and bank.
  3. Enter your account number or cryptocurrency wallet address and the amount you wish to withdraw.
  4. Tap the “WITHDRAW” button and enjoy your money!

How can I make use of your service for hotels and businesses to help them get access to affordable internet to drive their business.

Simply get a Wicrypt router and set it in your location. Visitors can download the Wicrypt app and access your data.

Can I convert my internet data back to cash?

Your internet data can only be converted to Wicrypt Credit.

Does Wicrypt provide the data I will sell?

Wicrypt provides a platform for ease of sharing data. You purchase this data from your local network provider.

Can I sell the WiFi from a personal router (not a Wicrypt router)?

You can share your hotspot via the mobile App if you do not have the router. Click here to download the App on Google Playstore or App Store.

How do I get the router?

Click on the ""Request a Router Button"" on the website. Fill the request form and our team will contact you within 24 hours.
